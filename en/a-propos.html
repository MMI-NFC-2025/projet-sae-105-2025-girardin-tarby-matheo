About

Welcome to this site dedicated to Lise Meitner, to the Matilda effect and to all too often forgotten women of science. My name is Mathéo Girardin Tarby, a first-year student in BUT MMi at Montbéliard.

Through this project, I wish to highlight those whose discoveries have marked our world, but whose names have been erased or ignored.

You will find here accessible, documented and committed articles that combine biography, historical analysis and popular culture. My goal is simple: to make these inspiring figures known, and contribute to a fairer representation in scientific narratives.

Thank you for your visit, and do not hesitate to contact me to discuss or propose ideas below!
